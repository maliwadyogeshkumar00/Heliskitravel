The Story

"You're not just booking a ski trip. You're asking someone to get this right."

I grew up racing on snow in Norway — two podium finishes at the Norwegian Junior National Ski Championships, training alongside the Junior National Ski Team. Skiing wasn't a hobby. It was how I understood the world.

After racing, I spent close to two decades in online travel and commercial media, before completing an MBA in Global Hospitality Management at Les Roches in Crans-Montana, Switzerland. I know what separates a good experience from an extraordinary one.

My connection to Viking Heliskiing goes back further than this business. Jóhann and Björgvin are people I've known from the skiing world for years. They come from elite ski racing, and that perfectionism hasn't left them — it's why the hospitality here is unlike anything else in the mountains.

Viking Heliskiing is a small, focused operation built around quality over volume. I know the packages, the weeks, the conditions, and what kind of skier belongs on which trip.
