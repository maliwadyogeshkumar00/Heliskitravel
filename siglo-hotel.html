The Hotel

Sigló Hotel.

One of Iceland's most celebrated boutique hotels, sitting directly on the harbour of Siglufjörður. Nordic design, Icelandic warmth, ocean views from every room, and access to the legendary outdoor hot tubs overlooking the fjord.

02

Fine Dining

Sunna Restaurant.

Sigló's harbourside restaurant serves a nightly three-course dinner inspired by North Icelandic ingredients — fresh-caught fish, local lamb, and seasonal produce. Intimate, candlelit, and utterly unhurried.

03

The Town

Siglufjörður.

A remote fishing town 40 kilometres north of the Arctic Circle, framed by towering mountains on three sides and the Greenland Sea beyond. Colourful, quiet, and genuinely unspoiled — one of Iceland's best-kept secrets.
