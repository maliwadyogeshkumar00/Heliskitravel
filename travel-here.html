Overview

A planned arrival.

Akureyri (AEY) is your gateway to northern Iceland. Most guests fly via Reykjavík (KEF); London Gatwick flies direct to Akureyri twice weekly.

London Gatwick operates a direct easyJet service to Akureyri (AEY) twice weekly (~3h 20min). All other routes connect via Reykjavík Keflavík (KEF) with a 40-min domestic flight onward to Akureyri. Geneva (GVA) direct service is seasonal (May–Sep). Flight times are approximate.
